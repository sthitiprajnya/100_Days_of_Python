New challenges don't bother me, but rather inspire, as they give me the opportunity to grow up and develop, and the habit of not give up helps me in this. Working through #100DaysOfCode.

Skills

My backstory

I have always been interested in how websites are created. I always wanted to impress my friends by writing programs, I managed to do this using the Telegram bot in a friendly chat. When I did this, the reverse side of the code struck me. Shortly thereafter, I signed up for a Python programming course at It-Academy. From that moment I fell in love with development. Until fall 2020, I mostly dabbled with Python, around October I started learning web development. The Django framework was opened shortly thereafter, and since then my love for development has skyrocketed. I am currently participating in 100 Days of Code - The Complete Python Pro Bootcamp for 2021.

So what's next?

At the time of this writing of this initial Gist, my goal was to get a job as a Python developer and start developing commercial Python applications, as well as develop a better understanding of Python, and develop a full stack. Although this is a big goal, I am willing to make every effort to achieve this goal.
